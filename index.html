Addressing Climate Change in the Arctic and Alaska

Addressing climate-related issues like melting permafrost, coastal erosion, subsistence hunting and gathering, food security, and freshwater resources is becoming increasingly important - both in the Arctic and Alaska at large. The resources provided below by the five Alaska Landscape Conservation Cooperatives highlight efforts to document and address these issues.
